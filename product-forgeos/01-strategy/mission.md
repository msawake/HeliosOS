# ForgeOS Mission

## Product Pitch
- **The "Two-Week Workflow" Platform:** We solve your most painful manual workflows with autonomous AI agents in 14 days or less.
- **Empowering Teams through the 90/10 Rule:** AI handles 90% of the repetitive execution so your best people can focus on the 10% of high-value judgment calls.
- **Trust-First Automation:** Multi-stack agent orchestration with ironclad human-in-the-loop governance and complete audit transparency.

## Target Users
### Primary User Personas
- **Operations Managers:** Needs to reduce operational bottlenecks and scaling costs without increasing headcount.
- **SME Business Owners:** Wants to modernize workflows and improve response times/accuracy but lacks a dedicated AI engineering team.
- **Department Leads (Finance/Sales/HR):** Needs specific tasks (invoicing, lead scoring, resume screening) handled reliably and securely.

## Core Problems Solved
- **Workflow Friction:** Traditional manual processes are slow, error-prone, and expensive to scale.
- **AI Trust Gap:** Businesses are afraid of "black box" AI making irreversible mistakes in financial or client-facing operations.
- **System Fragmentation:** Data is trapped in legacy systems (SAP, CRM, Email) without a bridge to modern AI reasoning.

## Differentiators
- **Managed "One-by-One" Implementation:** We don't sell a complex platform; we solve your specific pain points one workflow at a time.
- **Multi-Stack Flexibility:** Choose the best runtime for the job—ForgeOS native, CrewAI, Google ADK, or OpenClaw—all from a single control plane.
- **Graduated Autonomy:** Agents move from Shadow mode to Supervised to HITL-Gated, ensuring trust is earned, not assumed.

## Key Features
### DISCOVERY & DESIGN
- **Agent Wizard:** AI-assisted design of agent behaviors, tools, and prompts.
- **Skill Library:** 230+ pre-built domain expertise modules to ground agent reasoning.
- **Ontology Mapping:** Structured business knowledge to ensure agents understand your specific terminology.

### EXECUTION & ORCHESTRATION
- **Multi-Stack Adapters:** Seamless execution across multiple agent frameworks.
- **5 Execution Lifecycles:** Reflex, Scheduled, Event-Driven, Always-On, and Autonomous.
- **Workflow Engine:** DAG-based orchestration for complex, multi-step business processes.

### GOVERNANCE & TRUST
- **HITL Gateway:** Mandatory human approval for high-risk actions (financial, legal, client-facing).
- **7-Check Hook Chain:** Hard constraints on budget, rate limits, and tool permissions.
- **Audit Logging & Metrics:** Real-time visibility into token costs, accuracy, and agent activity.
