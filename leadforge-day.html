Autonomy Categories

Every agent action falls into one of four categories. The boundaries are absolute.

A — Fully Autonomous
No human involvement needed
  • Lead research & data enrichment
  • BANT scoring & qualification
  • CRM updates & pipeline reporting
  • System health monitoring
  • Marketing analytics & tracking
  • SEO optimization
  • Invoice generation & billing
Agents: sales-researcher, sales-scorer, sales-ops, mkt-analytics, mkt-seo, fin-ar, ops-monitoring
B — Autonomous + Audit
Agents act, 10-20% reviewed post-facto
  • Outbound email outreach (10% sample)
  • Nurture sequences (20% sample)
  • Google Ads bid adjustments (15% sample)
  • Content creation (compliance check)
  • Email marketing campaigns
  • Demand gen execution
Agents: sales-sdr, sales-nurture, mkt-ppc, mkt-content, mkt-email, mkt-demandgen
C — Pre-Approval Required
Agent drafts, human approves before execution
  • Client service agreements (48h SLA)
  • Ad spend increases >$500 (12h SLA)
  • Pricing discounts >15% (24h SLA)
  • New outreach channels (24h SLA)
  • Content publication (4h SLA)
  • Outreach compliance review (4h SLA)
Triggers from: legal-lead, mkt-ppc, sales-ae, sales-lead, mkt-content, legal-compliance
D — Human Only
No AI agent can perform these actions
  • Contract signing & legal execution
  • Regulatory & tax filings
  • Strategic pivots & new markets
  • Data breach response
  • Hiring / firing decisions
  • Client terminations
Handled by: Human board, legal counsel, CEO (human), CFO (human)
