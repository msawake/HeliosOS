Every AI action goes through 7 safety checks. Nothing happens without your rules being followed.

Your Rules, Enforced

You define the rules: "invoices over $10K need VP approval," "support replies must include disclaimer," "contracts with auto-renewal are always flagged." AI follows them every time, without forgetting.

AI never sends an email, approves a payment, or signs a contract without your team clicking "approve." We call this Human-in-the-Loop. It's non-negotiable.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action logged: what the AI did, why, what data it used, what the outcome was. Exportable. Auditor-ready. You always know what happened and why.

Your Data Is Yours

Completely isolated. Not shared with other clients. Not used to train models. Encrypted at rest and in transit. We can run on your infrastructure if required.
