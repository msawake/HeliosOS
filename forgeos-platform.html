Like Palantir's Foundry Ontology — a semantic layer that maps real-world business objects and their relationships.

Intelligence Use Cases

Churn Risk Analysis

USER: "Which customers are most at risk of churning?"
INTEL-ANALYST uses: ontology_query_objects(Customer, stage=active) → ontology_aggregate(Invoice, group_by=customer_id, metric=sum) → ontology_get_neighbors(customer, has_lead) → composes analysis

"🔴 HIGH RISK: DataFlow Inc — revenue down 40% QoQ, no new leads in 60 days. CloudBase — new contact hasn't engaged, invoice 30 days overdue. NexaTech — competitor mentioned in last call."

Pipeline Intelligence

USER: "What's the average deal size by industry and how does our pipeline look?"
INTEL-ANALYST uses: ontology_aggregate(Deal, group_by=industry, metric=avg, property=amount) → ontology_query_objects(Deal, stage=negotiation)

"Average deal size: Fintech $72K, Healthcare $45K, SaaS $38K. Pipeline: 12 deals in negotiation worth $580K total. 3 deals stalled >14 days — recommend follow-up."

Campaign ROI

USER: "Which campaigns generated the most qualified leads?"
INTEL-ANALYST uses: ontology_query_objects(Campaign, status=completed) → ontology_get_neighbors(campaign, generated_lead) → filters by lead score ≥70

"Top 3 by SQL generation: Q1 LinkedIn Outreach (23 SQLs, $12 CPL), Fintech Webinar (18 SQLs, $45 CPL), Google Ads Retarget (15 SQLs, $8 CPL). LinkedIn has best volume, Google Ads best unit economics."

Relationship Discovery

USER: "Show me everything connected to customer Acme Corp"
INTEL-ANALYST uses: ontology_search("Acme Corp") → ontology_get_neighbors(acme, depth=2)

"Acme Corp (Active, $2M revenue):
├── 5 leads (2 SQL, 3 MQL)
├── 2 active deals ($150K total)
├── 1 overdue invoice ($25K, 15 days late)
├── 3 campaigns targeting their leads
└── Next meeting: QBR on April 5"
